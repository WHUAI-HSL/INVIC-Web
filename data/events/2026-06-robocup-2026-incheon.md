# RoboCup 2026 HSL Call for Participation

Official invitation to apply for RoboCup 2026 Humanoid Soccer League in Incheon, South Korea.

## Event Details
- Date: June 30 - July 6, 2026
- Location: Incheon, South Korea
- League: Humanoid Soccer League (HSL)

## Divisions and Configurations
- Small Robot: height < 110 cm, weight < 15 kg
- Middle Robot: height < 125 cm, weight < 25 kg
- Large Robot: height < 190 cm, weight < 80 kg
- Foundation vs Advanced team configurations
  - Small: 4 (Foundation) / 7 (Advanced)
  - Middle: 3 (Foundation) / 5 (Advanced)
  - Large: 3 (Foundation) / 5 (Advanced)

## Application Timeline
- Submission system open: January 1, 2026
- Pre-application deadline: January 10, 2026
- Submission deadline: January 31, 2026
- Review period: February 3 - 20, 2026
- Qualified teams announced: March 2, 2026

## Key Submission Requirements
- Team information: name, homepage, contact, division, robot counts
- Qualification video (<= 5 minutes) provided as a link
- Team Description Paper (LNCS format, 4 pages)
- Software description form and hardware specification
- Letter of commitment
